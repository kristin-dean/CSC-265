Hello World

My name is Kristin Dean and my cause is my mother. My mom is the hardest-working and powerful woman I have ever met in my life and continues to be my inspiration and motivator every day. I can only hope to grow up to be like her someday and I notice our similarities more and more as I grow older.

One of the most obvious traits that my mom and I share are our academic interests. When I came to Centre and took math and computer science classes, I almost immediately knew I wanted to be a math major and computer science minor. Once I had decided this, I called my mom to let her know and that's when I found out she majored and minored in the exact same subjects when she was in college! I really enjoyed learning this because it made me feel like her, something I try to achieve every day.

Another reason I picked my mother to be my "cause" was due to her ability to overcome adversity with a smile. My mom was diagnosed with breast cancer for the first time in 2004, and then for the second time in 2010. While raising my five siblings and me and working a full-time job at Procter & Gamble, she overcame both of these challenges, even when she was broken down. This is why breast cancer research and data is a huge interest of mine and I am extremely lucky to have an internship this summer working in the Ohio State University Bioinformatics Department, working with breast cancer data and working with the R language to put this data into visualizations. This is also why I want to use breast cancer as my "cause" for data research!
